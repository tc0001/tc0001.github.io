About Us

Store Profile

  1. Our Stationery Store is a store that offer one stop stationery supplier is Malaysia,it is a special wholesale and supplier of stationery in Malaysia. Now, were looking forward to operate the branches for purpose upgrade our support and services to our customers.

  2. Our Stationery Store is going to hold brand for office stationery in Malaysia. Our store will produce some good quality products with the brand of our stationery store, our branding is to create awareness to our customer and to offer the high quality stationery products economic or cheap pricing.

  3. We practise family operating and management concept , hence we offer openness and trust in which every staff takes pride with the organisation and fells responsible for its success and well being.

  4. Our Stationery Store always believe in quality,we are dedicated to bring more unusual and creative products to consumer and customers, with continuous quality control and improvements, the store emphasizes on excellent in every products and works that it undertakes to provide good quality products in that we created to achieve a higher sales in all our stationery products and to ensure always being the special stationery in Malaysia.

Vision and Mission

  1. We plan to produce and provide the economical world class office stationery, office machine, service and support center for all our customers.

  2. With a full show room and display centre for the convenience of our customers to see and touch the products they wish to purchase, one of our goals is to be the client's first choice for our stationery products. Another goal is for our store to be unique in both retail and wholesale so that it can open additional locations in Malaysia.

  3. We constantly update our services and supports, improve the quality of logistics, and improve the delivery services in order to maintain our position as the leading stationery supplier in Malaysia.

  4. Our objectives are to offer the best services and conduct business in a special way that emphasizes growth and value, and serve as a one-stop shop for consumers and customers for all stationery needs.

  5. For our stationery store, we are committed to achieved these goals by serving the highest ethical and professional standards in how we do business and communicate with customers. We are also guided by the philosophy of offering the greatest services to each and every customers of our clients, as well as the best possible outcomes for the surrounding area and the community.

Product and Services

  1. From internationally recognised brands to our own stationery store brand, we provide all types of workplace stationery and children's stationery requirements.

  2. We also sell every form of printer, including Inkjet printers for personal and business usage, multifunction printers, fax machines, copier/photocopiers, and all types of cartridges for ink and toner in our stationery store.

  3. Furthermore, A full-service printing facility offering a wide range of printing projects, including rubber stamps, banners, letters, A4 papers, booklet, letterhead, and name cards. We also provide professional design services in our stationery store.
